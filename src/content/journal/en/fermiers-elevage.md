Cœurs Braves Fermiers develops modern, productive and sustainable agriculture. From egg production to livestock and food processing, the hub feeds communities while generating income.

## Structuring cooperatives

By bringing producers together in cooperatives, the hub pools resources, improves quality and opens access to markets. Agriculture becomes a real lever for local development.

## Training in agro-pastoral techniques

Cœurs Braves Fermiers trains people in modern farming and livestock techniques, so that every operation gains in productivity and autonomy.
