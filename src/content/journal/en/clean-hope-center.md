The Clean Hope Center embodies the Cœurs Braves way of acting: a unique place where training, craftsmanship, community health and support for the most vulnerable come together.

## Learn a trade

Sewing and crafts workshops allow participants to gain concrete skills and develop an income-generating activity. Every course opens up a new prospect.

## Take care

The center also hosts community health initiatives: awareness, prevention and support. Because human development begins with people's well-being.
