La Marmite des Cœurs Braves continues to grow with the opening of a new location in Sangmélima, in the South region. Much more than a kitchen, La Marmite puts hospitality at the service of solidarity, employment and living together.

## Feed and employ

Each location creates local jobs and trains young people in cooking and service. The meals prepared showcase produce from the Cœurs Braves Fermiers cooperatives, in a short-circuit, quality-focused approach.

## A place to meet

La Marmite is also a place of life and conviviality: a warm space — restaurant, bar and lounge — where people share a meal, talk and build bonds. It is this spirit, at the heart of communities, that gives the project all its meaning.

## The community's meeting point

Shared meals, reunions and gatherings: in Sangmélima, La Marmite quickly became a rallying point where the Cœurs Braves community meets, celebrates and moves forward together — "eat like at home".
