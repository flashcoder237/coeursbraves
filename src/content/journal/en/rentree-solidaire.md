For the new school year, the Cœurs Braves — together with the Hope Family association — visited the Oyack private social centre, as part of our programme to promote education for children from disadvantaged families and orphans.

## Donations and projects

Thanks to a collection carried out across our associations, we handed over school supplies and food donations to the centre. We also plan to launch training there in several fields — computing, agriculture, office skills, food processing — to help these people integrate socially and become self-reliant.

## Alongside families affected by disability

In Yaoundé, another visit focused on families with children living with a disability, to bring them some comfort at the start of the school year. What matters most is not the value of the gift, but the intention: **to give from the heart.**
