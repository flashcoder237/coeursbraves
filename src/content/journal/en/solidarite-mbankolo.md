The Cœurs Braves of Yaoundé went to Nkomkana to hand over donations collected for Cameroonians affected by the tragedy that struck Mbankolo.

These families, who lost everything — even their homes —, now need almost everything. At such a time, every gesture, however small, can make a difference.

Thank you to all the Cœurs Braves for their mobilisation. Because solidarity takes on its full meaning when it responds to an emergency, alongside those who have lost everything.
