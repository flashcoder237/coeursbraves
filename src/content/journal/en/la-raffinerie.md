Part of Cœurs Braves Academy, La Raffinerie is a premium programme of social and relational transformation. Its ambition: to reveal human potential beyond technical skills alone.

## Reveal yourself

Personal development, charisma, self-confidence, personal image: the programme guides everyone towards better self-knowledge and a confident presence.

## Shine

Human relationships, social elegance, emotional education and professional conduct: as many keys to building strong relationships and moving with assurance in every setting.
