Here is what plastic bottles can also be used for. Many people don't know it: when reused well, they become useful everyday resources.

## Turning waste into value

From soilless growing to everyday objects, upcycling opens up concrete ways to reduce waste while creating value. A simple approach, within reach of every community.

## Raise awareness, take action

Cœurs Braves encourages these ecological and community initiatives, because sustainable development begins with everyday gestures and the sharing of knowledge.
