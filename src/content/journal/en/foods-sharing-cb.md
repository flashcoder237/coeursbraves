Together with the members of the association, we ran a food-donation drive at the New-Deido market in Douala, for young children and elderly women, to help meet their needs at the start of the year.

## Supporting the vendors

We also bought the entire stock of several elderly women, to support them and encourage them never to give up despite life's hardships.

## A monthly appointment

These philanthropic actions, known as **"Foods Sharing CB"**, continue every month in various markets across Cameroonian cities. Because solidarity is lived on the ground, as close as possible to people.
