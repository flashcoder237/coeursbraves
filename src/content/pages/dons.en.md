## Why donate

Cœurs Braves is an independent ecosystem. Every donation funds an
operational programme directly:

- **Academy scholarships** — full training coverage for one candidate
  with no resources.
- **The Refinery** — premium programme access for selected profiles.
- **Cœurs Braves Association** — community initiatives on the ground
  (Cameroon, diaspora).

## How to donate

Three channels at this time:

1. **Bank transfer** (IBAN on request at `donate@coeurs-braves.com`)
2. **Mobile money** — Orange Money, MTN MoMo (Cameroon)
3. **Credit card** — via a secure link shared after first contact

## Transparency

A quarterly allocation report is published in the Journal. For donations
above €1,000, a personalised tax receipt is issued.
