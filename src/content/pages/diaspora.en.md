## The network

Our Verein branches (Germany) and local chapters (Paris, Brussels,
Montréal — upcoming) bring the diaspora together around meetings,
workshops and mentor calls.

## Diaspora programmes

- **Mentorship** — talent from the continent supported by experienced
  diaspora members.
- **Patient capital** — co-financing through the Investor desk.
- **Productive return** — individual coaching for returns to the
  country of origin.
