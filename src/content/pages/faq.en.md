## About the ecosystem

**Is Cœurs Braves a company, an association, a foundation?**

It's a multi-entity ecosystem: association in Cameroon, Verein in
Germany, operational branches specialised per world. Common governance
sits with the board of directors.

## About The Refinery

**How do I apply?**

Via the form in [The Refinery](#raffinerie) section on the home page.
Sessions run by cohort (Abidjan, Dakar, Kigali…).

**What does it cost?**

Variable by cohort. Partial or full scholarships available on
application.

## About donations

**Is my donation tax-deductible?**

Yes in several jurisdictions depending on the channel. A tax receipt is
issued on request for donations above €1,000.
